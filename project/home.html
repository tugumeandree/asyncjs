About DayStar Daycare

DayStar Daycare is a prominent daycare facility located in Kabalagala. With an average of 150 babies a day attended by well-trained 'Sitters'.

The daycare receives supplies needed for babies such as diapers, fruits, milk among others and the school runs a 'Dolls' stall.

Sitter Requirements

  • Must stay around Kabalagala
  • Paid daily a fee of 3000ugx per babies they attend to
  • Registered once at school with details like Names, Location, Date of Birth, Gender, Next of Kin, NIN, Recommender's Name, Religion (Optional), level of education, sitter number, and contacts.
  • Off-duty sitters should not be selected to attend to babies
